About us

Sneaker beast was established in May 2019. We are an exclusive footwear customisation
brand, creating unique designs and adding vibrant enhancements to classic silhouettes that
can't be purchased on the high street or anywhere else.

It all started as a hobby, so Matt could have shoes that were unique to him and that no one
else had. MattB Customs is now a team of 10, working from a studio based in
Manchester, UK
